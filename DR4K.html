DEATH ROOM 4000

RELEASE DATE: DECEMBER 2016

LUDUM DARE ENTRY PAGE

After meeting a programmer friend on another project, he told me that Ludum Dare was coming up, and asked me if I'd like to collaborate with him on an entry. I agreed immediately, and looked forward to the theme announcement. After deciding on a concept, we set out to create a hardcore puzzle platformer.

Creating an entire game from scratch over 72 hours is quite an experience. It was only possible with the work of my team mate, who ended up creating the absolutely sadistic level design, presumably because he wasn't hugged enough when we was a child (although that's just speculation on my part).

We submitted our game at the last minute, after negotiating itch.io webhosting (you can play that version here ).
