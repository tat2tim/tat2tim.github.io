MALBONO MANOR

RELEASE DATE: OCTOBER 2016

PLAY IT!

My first game! Originally released on iOS. My first effort at making a game was actually a retro-style Metal Gear parody called 'Sneak Shot'. After playing one of the Ys games on the Virtual Console I decided to start from scratch, using a similar battle system, because I thought it would work better on the mobile platform. Honestly, I never felt the touch controls were great, and I think the game is much better like this, as it's more true to the retro experience than playing it on a phone. (although having an app on the app store is neat).

I've always been a fan of horror themed games from the early 8-bit era, and the haunted house theme (and vastly simplified gameplay) were a much better fit for my creative process and programming skill level at the time. I'm a bit ashamed to say that after years of criticizing larger devs for rushing products to market...I rushed my product to market. I was just super excited about having a real game released on a real platform, and I could download it to my own phone!
